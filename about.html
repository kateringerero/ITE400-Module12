About me

Front End Developer

Hello! I'm Katherine, a passionate Front End Developer with a flair for creating engaging and user-friendly digital experiences. With a background in computer science and a deep love for web design, I've dedicated my career to the art and science of website development.

My journey began at PHINMA University of Pangasinan - Dagupan, where I honed my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Since then, I've embraced the rapid evolution of front-end technologies, mastering frameworks like React and Vue.js to bring dynamic, responsive, and visually appealing interfaces to life.

In my current role at ABC Tech, I collaborate with cross-functional teams, translating design concepts into functional website features. I pride myself on my meticulous attention to detail and my commitment to writing clean, efficient, and accessible code.

When I'm not coding, I love contributing to open-source projects, staying ahead of the latest industry trends, and sharing my knowledge with budding developers at local meetups and tech conferences. I'm always excited about new challenges and opportunities to push the boundaries of what's possible on the web. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
